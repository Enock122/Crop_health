Agricultural Tips & Best Practices

  • Water early morning or evening to reduce evaporation
  • Use drip irrigation for water efficiency
  • Check soil moisture before watering
  • Mulch to retain moisture
  • Collect rainwater for irrigation

  • Practice crop rotation to break pest cycles
  • Use integrated pest management (IPM)
  • Encourage beneficial insects
  • Remove infected plants promptly
  • Use organic pesticides when possible
  • Monitor regularly for early detection

  • Add organic matter regularly
  • Practice crop rotation
  • Avoid over-tilling
  • Use cover crops
  • Test soil annually
  • Maintain proper pH levels

  • Choose varieties suited to your climate
  • Plan planting based on rainfall patterns
  • Maintain crop diversity
  • Keep detailed farm records
  • Plan for succession planting
  • Consider market demand

  • Clean tools after each use
  • Sharpen cutting tools regularly
  • Store equipment in dry places
  • Service machinery before planting season
  • Check irrigation systems for leaks
  • Maintain proper inventory of spare parts
